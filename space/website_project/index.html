What We Predict

Our AI models predict the following key space weather events:

  • Solar Flares: Sudden eruptions of radiation from the sun's surface that can disrupt satellite communications and navigation systems.
  • Geomagnetic Storms: Disturbances in the Earth's magnetosphere caused by solar wind and solar flares, potentially leading to power outages and communication disruptions.

Why Predict Space Weather?

Space weather events can have serious consequences on Earth and in space. By predicting these events, organizations can take preventive actions to avoid damage to critical infrastructure. Key reasons to predict space weather include:

  • Infrastructure Protection: Safeguarding satellites and power grids from potential disruptions caused by solar flares and geomagnetic storms.
  • Enhanced Safety: Improving the safety of space missions and ensuring the reliability of communication networks.
  • Informed Decision-Making: Providing data-driven insights that help industries make informed decisions to mitigate risks associated with space weather.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Allowing organizations to develop contingency plans for severe space weather events, ensuring quick responses.
